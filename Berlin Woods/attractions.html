Local Attractions

Everything Amish Country has to offer

Berlin Woods puts you in the middle of one of Ohio's most visited destinations. Shops, bakeries, live entertainment, and open countryside are all within a few minutes of your stay.

Near Berlin, Ohio

A full day out, then back to the quiet

Berlin is a small town with an outsized amount to do. Most guests spend their days exploring Amish Country and their evenings unwinding back at the property — the best of both worlds in one short drive.

Entertainment

Live shows, culture, and classic Amish Country experiences

Amish Country Theater

A 600-seat family variety show featuring comedians, ventriloquists, and the Amish comedy trio The Beachy's. Voted a Top 100 Event in North America. Located inside the Berlin Encore Hotel lobby.

Downtown Berlin · amishcountrytheater.com

Behalt Cyclorama

A 10-foot tall, 265-foot circular oil painting depicting the history of the Amish and Mennonite people. One of the most unique cultural experiences in Ohio.

Berlin · About 5 minutes away

Horse-Drawn Buggy Rides

Take a guided buggy ride through the Amish countryside and learn about the culture from local guides. A memorable experience for all ages and a genuine glimpse into daily Amish life.

Available locally in Berlin area
Dining

Family-style meals, homemade pies, and local favorites

Boyd & Wurthmann Restaurant

A Berlin institution since 1938. Known for homemade pies, fresh-baked bread, and classic Amish-style comfort food. Expect a line — it's worth it.

Downtown Berlin · Less than 1 mile

Berlin Farmstead Restaurant

Family-style Amish cooking with hearty portions, fried chicken, and homemade sides. A favorite for groups and families looking for an authentic local meal.

Berlin · Less than 1 mile

Blue Sky Cafe

A go-to spot in Berlin for a relaxed meal and a friendly atmosphere. A solid local pick any time of day.

Berlin · Less than 1 mile
Shopping

Amish-made goods, specialty shops, and small-batch favorites

Heini's Cheese Chalet

One of Ohio's most visited cheese shops, with over 50 varieties made on-site. Free samples, a viewing window into the production floor, and a great selection of local goods.

Berlin · About 2 minutes away

Yoder's Amish Home

A working Amish farm with guided tours, a gift shop, and genuine insight into Amish daily life. A popular stop for first-time visitors to Holmes County.

Millersburg · About 10 minutes away

Downtown Berlin Shops

Berlin's main street is lined with Amish-made furniture, handmade quilts, candles, fudge, and specialty shops. Plan to spend at least a half day browsing.

Downtown Berlin · Less than 1 mile
Outdoor Activities

Trails, countryside views, and time outside

Holmes County Trail

A paved multi-use trail running through the heart of Holmes County — a peaceful way to explore the countryside by bike or on foot, passing through Amish farmland and small towns.

Accessible from Berlin area

Killbuck Marsh Wildlife Area

One of Ohio's largest inland marshes and a prime spot for birdwatching, especially during spring and fall migration. Canoe access and walking trails available.

About 15 minutes away

Scenic Countryside Drives

Holmes County's back roads wind through working Amish farms, rolling hills, and one-room schoolhouses. Roll the windows down and take your time — there's no better way to take it all in.

Starts right outside your door
